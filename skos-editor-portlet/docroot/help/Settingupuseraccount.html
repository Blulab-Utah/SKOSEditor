Setting up user account

Using SKOS Editor ››

The account set up process is straightforward.  Please go to:


http://blulab2.chpc.utah.edu:8080/web/guest/skos


And click on the blue “Create Account” button in the middle of the screen




Then fill in your account details.  Only the “First Name”, “Email Address”, and “Text Verification” are required.



Then enter the email address and password supplied to log in.
